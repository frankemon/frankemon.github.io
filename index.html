What have I done so far?

Most recently, I've worked as a consultant for Alphabyte AB, where I was assigned to Foggy Media AB. My task was to build a SaaS, code testing platform with the goal of releasing their product for school systems and recruiters alike. While this product is not live yet, it's built with Typescript, NextJS, Redux and Strapi, a Javascript based headless CMS for the backend.

I've interned at Chesshotel, an online chess platform, for over a year. Their platform is built with Typescript using React, NextJS and Redux on the frontend. The backend uses Koa/NodeJS and TypeORM to connect to a mySQL DB. I helped develop a new game mode, correspondence chess: turn-based gameplay where users have up to two days to complete their turns.

In my downtime I've also freelanced for Deskhunters AB, a Stockholm based startup that provides instant workplace solutions by offering pay-as-you-go, rentable desks at many different locations throughout the city. My task here building the website and structuring the CMS to allow for easily maintainable, dynamic content based on their needs. This product was built in Webflow.

I've also interned at Starring AB, a web agency working with WordPress offering affordable digital solutions to businesses. My task at Starring AB was to migrate several websites to new domains by recreating them and preparing them for their new digital homes.
